Recent Professional Activities

Prof. Kui Ren is a Fellow of IEEE, a Distinguished Lecturer of IEEE Vehicular Technology Society, Member of ACM, Member of Sigma Xi, and a past Board Member of Internet Privacy Task Force, State of Illinois.

He currently serves on the Editorial Boards of

  • ACM Transactions on Cyber-Physical Systems (TCPS)
  • Elsevier Computer Networks
  • ZTE Communications
  • IEEE Transactions on Service Computing (TSC)
  • SpringerBriefs on Cyber Security Systems and Networks, Springer
  • IEEE Transactions on Dependable and Secure Computing (TDSC)
  • IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing (TMC)
  • IEEE Internet of Things Journal (IoT-J)
  • IEEE Wireless Communications (WCM)
  • Chinese Journal of Electronics (CJE)
  • Chinese Journal of Network and Information Security (CJNIS)
  • CCF Transactions on Networking

And he was an Associate Editor for

  • IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid (TSG)
  • IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS)
  • Oxford The Computer Journal
  • Elsevier Pervasive and Mobile Computing (PMC)
  • Journal of Communications and Networks (JCN)

He is a Steering Committee Member of ACM AsiaCCS'19.

He is a TPC Co-Chair of IWQoS'20, TPS'19, APSys'19, DSC'19，ICDCS'17 (Algorithm and Theory Track), PAC'17, Securecomm'17, and Globecom'17 (Security Track).

He is involved with the following conferences: ICDCS'20, DBSec'20, ESORICS'20, SenSys'19, AsiaCCS'18, AsiaCCS'17, ESORICS'17, CLOUD'17,...
